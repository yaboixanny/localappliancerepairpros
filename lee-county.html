Got Questions? We've Got Answers

We handle most major household appliances, including refrigerators, freezers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, cooktops, washers, dryers, and more. If you are unsure whether we service your appliance, just give us a call and we can let you know quickly.

Do you offer appliance repair in Lee County and surrounding areas?

Yes. We focus on Lee County and nearby communities so we can provide faster scheduling, dependable arrival windows, and local service you can count on.

How soon can I get an appointment?

In many cases, same-day or next-day appointments may be available depending on the schedule and the type of repair needed. The best way to check availability is to call or submit the form with your appliance issue.

What kinds of appliance problems do you fix?

We help with many of the most common issues homeowners run into, including refrigerators not cooling, dryers not heating, washers not spinning, dishwashers not draining, ovens not heating, strange noises, leaks, and other performance problems.

What should I have ready when I call for service?

It helps to know the appliance type, brand, model if available, and what symptoms you are seeing. If you already have photos or an error code, that can make it even easier to understand the issue before the appointment.
